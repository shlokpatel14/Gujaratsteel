Who We Are

Two Decades of Steel
Expertise in Gujarat

Gujarat Steel was established in 2010 by Manish Patel and Sharad Patel as a partnership firm dedicated to serving the industrial and manufacturing sector. From our base in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we have grown into a reliable name for steel trading across India.

Our focus has always been simple: provide quality steel products, maintain a dependable supply chain, and build long-term relationships with our customers. Whether you are a machine manufacturer, fabricator, or engineering firm, we understand your material requirements and deliver accordingly.

With over two decades in the business, we carry deep knowledge of steel grades, their applications, and what your project demands. That knowledge, combined with competitive pricing and consistent availability, makes Gujarat Steel the supplier businesses come back to.

What We Supply

Our Steel Products

From standard mild steel to high-carbon alloy grades — we stock what your business needs, ready to supply.

MS Steel

Mild Steel for general structural and fabrication applications. Widely used in construction, machine parts, and infrastructure projects across industries.

Mild Steel

VSP Steel

Vizag Steel (VSP) structural and TMT products known for their consistent quality and mechanical properties. Suitable for heavy engineering and construction.

Structural

EN8 Steel

Medium carbon steel offering good tensile strength and toughness. Ideal for shafts, axles, gears, studs, and general engineering components requiring moderate strength.

Alloy Grade

EN9 Steel

Higher carbon steel with superior hardness and wear resistance compared to EN8. Used for crankshafts, connecting rods, high-stress machine parts, and tools.

Alloy Grade

EN19 Steel

Chromium-molybdenum alloy steel providing excellent strength, toughness, and hardenability. Used in heavy-duty shafts, gears, bolts, and high-pressure components.

Alloy Grade

EN31 Steel

High-carbon chromium bearing steel with outstanding wear resistance and hardness. The preferred choice for ball bearings, roller bearings, and precision tooling applications.

Bearing Grade

Bright Bars

Cold-drawn and precision-finished bright steel bars with tight dimensional tolerances and superior surface finish. Available in round, square, flat, and hex profiles.

Finished Bar

Round Bars

Hot-rolled and bright round bars in various diameters and steel grades. Versatile for shafts, pins, fasteners, and machining applications across engineering industries.

Bar Stock

Square Bars

Square section steel bars for structural framing, keys, keyways, jigs, and fixtures. Available in mild steel and alloy grades to suit your application requirements.

Bar Stock

Hex Bars

Hexagonal section bright bars for nuts, bolts, valve components, and precision turned parts. Saves material and machining time in fastener and fitting production.

Bar Stock

Pata Patti

Flat steel strips (Pata Patti) in standard and custom widths. Widely used in fabrication, binding, clamping, automotive body work, and general industrial applications.

Flat Strip

Ruff Pata Patti

Rough-edged flat steel strips with a hot-rolled surface finish. Cost-effective for applications where exact dimensions are less critical — used in structural and fabrication work.

Flat Strip
